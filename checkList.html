Safety Check List

A list of essentials to keep in your car during all seasons
in case of emergency or breakdown of car

  • Hazard Triangle
  • Hi-vis bib
  • Insurance company contact details
  • Replacement blubs and fuses
  • Spare tires and jack
  • Mobile phone and charger
  • Road map
  • First-aid kit
  • Water
  • Non-perishable food
  • Loose change
  • Umbrella
  • Waterproof clothing
  • Steel-toe boots
  • Torch
  • Spade
  • Jump cable
  • Extra fuel can
